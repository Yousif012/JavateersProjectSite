User Stories

1: Creating Equations

As a student, I want to be able to write complex equations into my notes.

Elaboration: Users should be able to quickly and easily create a wide range of equations and put them in their notes. It should be easy to do to avoid confusion and quick enough that students can keep up while writing notes during a lecture.

Constraints: none

Effort Estimation: 8 person-hours

Acceptance Test: Create five different equations of increasing complexity using a built-in tool and ensure they all render correctly.
