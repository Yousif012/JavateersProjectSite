1. Introduction

1.1 Purpose of Product

The purpose of our product is to allow users to take notes easily with zero to little limitations to how users want to jot notes. Anyone should be able to record any information with our product and be able to organize their ideas so that it is easy to go back and review any information. Being able to record complex information such as chemical and mathematical formulas should be fast and easy so that students can keep up with a lecture when writing notes.

1.2 Scope of Product

Users should be able to take notes easily. This product is not meant to be a knowledge management platform or a database. This product is designed with students in mind and as such all features are implemented with taking notes during a lecture in mind. For example, students sometimes need to record a table of data in their notes. We will have basic table functionality. However, our product is not a spreadsheet program so any complex functionality such as a sum of cells, spreadsheet automation, math functions created from a selection of cells, etc. are not within the scope of our product.

2.2 Domain Model with Description

Our product's domain is note-taking. Within this domain, the problem we are attempting to solve with our product is that it is oftentimes restricting when taking notes on a computer or mobile device when compared to taking notes with pen and paper. With our product, you will be able to take notes anywhere on the "page", draw anywhere on the "page", make tables, charts and graphs, write complex mathematical formulae, and plot mathematical functions. All of this you will be able to do quickly and easily so you can focus on learning and listening, rather than fiddling with a complex program.
