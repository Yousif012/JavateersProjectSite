Problem Statement

Introduction

In today's fast-paced world, people are constantly bombarded with information from
multiple sources such as meetings, classes, and daily tasks. With so much to keep track of, it
can be difficult to remember everything, and important details can easily slip through the
cracks. Traditional note-taking methods such as pen and paper or sticky notes can be
inconvenient, disorganized, and easily misplaced. This can lead to frustration and
inefficiency, especially for those who rely on accurate and timely note-taking to
succeed in their personal and professional lives.

To address this issue, we the Javateers will propose a solution that can solve this issue. This
solution will take the form of a note taking app. This app will allow users to easily write down
organize information, set reminders, and have a reliable second brain for accessing critical details.
This app will not only save you time, but also save you money as the old saying goes, time is money.

Features

One of the key features of our app is the ability to create both typed and drawn notes.
Whether you prefer to jot down quick notes with your keyboard or sketch out your ideas using a stylus, our app can handle it all.
Our drawing tool offers a range of brushes and colors to choose from, so you can create notes that are as unique as your ideas.
Another great feature of our app is the ability to organize your notes in a way that makes sense to you.
With the option to create multiple notebooks, you can keep your notes separated by project, subject, or any other category you choose.
Plus, with the ability to tag your notes, you can easily find specific notes later on.


Our app also offers a variety of customization options to make your note-taking experience even more personal.
You can choose from a range of fonts and font sizes to suit your preferences, as well as adjust the color scheme to match your style.
In addition to these features, our app offers a variety of other tools to make your note-taking experience more efficient.
You can set reminders for specific notes, create to-do lists, and even collaborate with others in real-time.
Overall, our note-taking app is the perfect tool for anyone who wants to capture their ideas quickly and easily, whether through typing or drawing.
With a range of customization options and organizational tools, our app is designed to help you stay on top of your notes and stay productive.

Tool Used

Android Studio is the official integrated development environment (IDE) for developing Android applications.
It is based on IntelliJ IDEA, a Java integrated development environment for software development, and includes its code editing and developer tools.
Android Studio is a unified development environment that allows you to create apps for Android phones, tablets, Android Wear, Android TV, and Android Auto.
Structured code modules allow you to divide your project into functional units that you can build, test, and debug independently.
We chose Java as a mobile app development language because it has many advantages and reasons to be used. Currently,
the primary advantages of using Java for mobile app development range from cross-platform compatibility, constant updates,
and open-source to community support and native mobile development tools.
